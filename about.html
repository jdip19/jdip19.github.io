About Me

The Beginning

Jogi Infotech & The Spark of Creativity

I began my journey in 2021 as a graphic designer and WordPress developer at Jogi Infotech. At the time, I was handling random projects, but I found myself drawn toward creative design — especially after seeing inspiring posts on Instagram. That passion led me to start a page called The Graphic Fortress, where I shared my thoughts and ideas through creative graphics.

Going Solo

The Only Designer at Elluminati Inc.

After about 10 months, I applied for the role of Graphic & UI Designer at Elluminati Inc., Rajkot. Out of a 60-person team, I was the only designer — which forced me to adapt quickly and learn how to work in a collaborative environment. Initially, my work revolved around app logos and social media banners, but I slowly transitioned into UI/UX design.

Automation

Learning Through ChatGPT

Around that time, ChatGPT launched — and it changed the game for me. With its help, I learned how to automate repetitive tasks and focus on what really matters. For example, as I was managing tiffin (lunch) orders at the office, I built a simple Tiffin Management web app using Firebase and GitHub to streamline the process — all with the help of my developer friends and ChatGPT. After contributing in multiple roles, I decided to focus purely on being a UI/UX designer.

Breaking Blocks

Evolving as a Web & Product Designer

With a warm farewell, I left Elluminati & Good Buddies after 1.5 years and joined Uptechmind. This time, I moved from a product-based to a service-based company. The shift came with its own challenges — I had to push myself harder to understand varied client requirements and turn them into real products. At first, I felt creatively blocked, but over time, my understanding of website design deepened. I began to recognize what truly makes a product both aesthetic and usable.
